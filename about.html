About Us
WELCOME YOU ALL TO THE WORLD OF SOAPS & COSMETICS.  

For many, “SOAP” is just one aspect of daily grooming but for us, it’s to the body, what laughter is to the soul. It is therapeutic. It has to be natural, nourishing and utterly pampering with healing benefits.

Our aim here is to create a unique and natural cleansing experience that leaves the skin completely nourished and hydrated. We take pride in creating organic handmade soaps with extra Organic olive oil, Coconut Oils, Herbs and botanicals, using the traditional cold process method.

Most of our soaps are pure extra virgin olive oil-based, made the traditional way. What makes olive oil stand out in skincare is the high percentage of the good oleic acid versus a low ratio of linoleic acid. Its light molecular structure makes it suitable for all skin types. Olive oil deeply conditions the skin, prevents the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines, and even treats atopic eczema in kids. To ensure that optimal efficacy of this ingredient, we source the purest form of olive oil from some of the best cultivation in India. It is the preferred carrier oil in aromatherapy as it doesn’t have any distinct aroma of its own.

Since the Olive Oil based Soaps are quiet expensive, we also have 100 % coconut oil soaps. Besides cleansing and moisturizing benefits, the coconut oil based soaps have antibacterial, anti-fungal and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to deal with many kinds of skin issues. Soaps made with coconut oil also provide a nice bubbly lather naturally.  Since each type of oil brings different properties to the final soap, we also have our signature blends to provide maximum conditioning and cleansing benefits.

What’s more? Our soaps smell divine! While the common advice is to find soap as per your skin type, we nudge you to go a step further, and find a soap whose fragrance is in sync with your mind. The sense of smell is most intimately linked to the deepest parts of the brain, which directly controls emotions.  As we inhale certain aromas of essential oils, it triggers an emotional response. For instance, the smell of roses is uplifting, while lavender is relaxing.

We experiment with a range of shapes, colours and aromas to transform your daily baths to indulgent rituals. Each bar of soap is carefully hand finished and then packaged. So, pamper your skin with our nourishing natural soaps and feel the difference.

OUR JOURNEY

“Life is full of beauty, one just has to notice it”.

Everyone has a story of how it all began and this is MINE, short and sweet.  
Hello Friends, I'm Sirisha Aditya, founder of “Hearty Bubble” soaps.

I am a Management Graduate from Symbiosis Institute of Business Management Pune with 2 decades of hard-core experience in Business Management. This was in 2018. What started as a hobby became a stress-buster. When I thought of making my own soaps for personal use, I learnt about the differences between commercial soaps and natural soaps, how the commercial soaps have the harsh effects on skin with unsafe chemicals. So I started exploring and learning ways of making handmade soaps which are 100% natural ingredients.

Every day would be a new experiment with oils, aromas and textures. My family and friends were more than happy to test new samples every week and there was quiet a good positive feedback and it turned into a small business. That’s when the Hearty Bubble Soap was born.

What makes It Awesome?

All our natural soaps are handmade from scratch, in small quantities, with a combinations of cold pressed coconut oil, olive oil, castor oil, almond oil, Shea butter, mango butter, cocoa butter, kokum butter, mow rah butter using a cold process method. Natural essential oils of plants and flowers are used for fragrance. Also, natural colorants like clays, herbs, charcoal are used which also have good effects on skin. The soaps are air dried and cured for a minimum of four weeks to produce a natural, mild and gentle bar. They are free from harmful synthetic fragrances, sles, paraben, artificial colouring agents.

During this learning process, I was literally in love with the magical way of converting oils and butters to soaps (This process is called as saponification). I really enjoy making soaps with design out to 100% natural colours.

Our aim is to create a unique and natural cleansing experience that leaves the skin completely nourished and hydrated. So, pamper your skin with our nourishing natural soaps and feel the difference.
