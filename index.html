About Us

Dhruva Enterprises

At Dhruva Enterprises, we are dedicated to providing a range of eco-friendly and sustainable products that embody the essence of nature's goodness. Our journey began with a passion for creating high-quality handmade items that promote well-being and sustainability.

Founded by Sirisha Aditya, our commitment to crafting natural products stems from a deep love for the environment and a desire to offer alternatives free from harmful chemicals. Inspired by the beauty of nature, our products are carefully handmade in small batches using a blend of nourishing ingredients such as coconut oil, olive oil, and essential oils.

FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Does your Soap contain Palm Oil?

No, Whe tend to use high quality butters and oils.

2. Please Tell us about Lathering in Soap?

Yes, Our soaps do lather like every other soap.

3. What is the Expiry Date for Soaps?

The soaps can be used within 6 months as we don't use any kind of preservatives. It can be used upto an year but there could be change in colour of the soap.

4. How long does the Soap last?

The 100 gms soap can be used in around 25 to 30 baths approximately when used by one person. But when one soap is used by two to three people, it might last less than the mentioned time period.

5. What is the delivery charge?

Delivery is absolutely "FREE" if the overall billing is above 699/- within Hyderabad city. If the bill is less than that, standard delivery charges would be applicable. 

Similarly, orders above 4000/- would be dispatched free for interstate and orders above 10,000/- would be dispatched free internationally.

All orders will be confirmed and dispatched the next day of the order.

6. How will my ordered items be delivered to me?

Our delivery executives will deliver your order on your doorstep.
